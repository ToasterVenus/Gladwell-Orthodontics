This is a windows app to keep track of your movies, tv series, and video games.


Features


Sign in to your profile to track your items.

Build your lists.

Create your likes for a item, review, or list.

Add a wish to the wishlist.

Get notifacations from the website to see your friends.

Upgrade to Pro or VIP to see comprehensive stats.

Build a large collection of movies, series, and video games as you grow!




Similar than Letterboxd, or Backloggd.
